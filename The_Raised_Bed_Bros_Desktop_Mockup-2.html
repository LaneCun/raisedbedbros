Our Story

We grew up on a farm in a big family where hard work wasn’t optional — it was part of everyday life. Early mornings, dirt on our hands, and building things side by side shaped who we are. Out there, we learned something simple but powerful: if you want something to grow, you build the right foundation first. Years later, we brought those same lessons into backyards. We design and build strong 8x4 cedar raised beds that bring families back outside and back together. We’re not just building garden beds. We’re building spaces where families grow together.
